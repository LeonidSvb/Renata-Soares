Teacher Answer Key: Lesson 3 RFS Worksheet -- Personal Connection and Writing

Lesson Plan Guidance: This key covers the yes/no comprehension questions (Page 7) and personal birthday drawing (Page 8). Q1 and Q3 are YES. Q2 is YES (brown paper, not birthday paper). The drawing is personal -- accept any birthday-related image with at least one written word.

📖 EXERCISE 1: READ, FIND AND WRITE — Answers
1. Is Jack's birthday today?
The student should write: YES
Text Evidence: "Today is Jack's birthday." (Page 5)
2. Is the box wrapped in brown paper?
The student should write: YES
Text Evidence: "It is covered in brown paper." (Page 5)
3. Does Jack find a bright red boat inside?
The student should write: YES
Text Evidence: "There is a bright red boat inside." (Page 5)
🎨 EXERCISE 2: DRAW AND LABEL — Answer
Draw YOUR birthday! Show something special from YOUR birthday day.
The student's drawing should show: Any birthday scene -- cake, gifts, friends, or party decorations. Accept all personal drawings.
Label 1 (I drew:): Accept any birthday-related noun (cake, gift, friends, party, candles)
Label 2 (It is special because:): Accept any personal reason -- invented spelling is OK at this level
