Teacher Answer Key: Lesson 4 RFS Worksheet + Quick Check (Page 10) -- Jack's Birthday

Lesson Plan Guidance: This key covers the Lesson 4 review worksheet AND the Quick Check on Page 10. Q1: 'big' (RF.1.2). Q2: 'cover' (L.1.4 multiple meaning word). Q3: 'a bright red boat' (RL.1.1). Quick Check Q4: 'What day is it?' (title comprehension). Accept all phonetically reasonable spellings.

📖 EXERCISE 1: READ, FIND AND WRITE — Answers
1. Which word starts with the /b/ sound: big, hat, or dog?
The student should write: big
Text Evidence: "He sees a big box in his room." (Page 5) -- big starts with /b/.
2. What word from the story can mean TWO different things?
The student should write: cover
Text Evidence: "It is covered in brown paper." (Page 5) -- cover = lid/wrap. Also = front of a book.
3. What was inside Jack's big box?
The student should write: a bright red boat
Text Evidence: "There is a bright red boat inside." (Page 5)
🎨 EXERCISE 2: DRAW AND LABEL — Answer
Draw Jack's birthday box and what was inside. Label 2 things!
The student's drawing should show: The big box (wrapped in brown paper) with a bright red boat visible inside
Label 1 (What Jack found:): a bright red boat (accept: boat)
Label 2 (The box was covered in:): brown paper (accept: brown)
