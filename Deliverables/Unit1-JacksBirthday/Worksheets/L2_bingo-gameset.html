Teacher's Manual: Homograph Match-Up Bingo 🎲
Goal of the Game
Students match vocabulary words from Page 6 Word Work to their bingo cards. Reinforces RF.1.2 and L.1.4 homograph vocabulary.
How to Play
  1. Teacher draws a calling card and reads the word and clue aloud.
  2. Students find the word on their bingo card and cover it with a marker.
  3. First student to cover 3 in a row shouts BINGO! and reads the 3 words to confirm.
Teacher Script 🗣️
Word Explorers, bingo time! I'll call out a word and a clue. If you know the word, find it on your card and cover it. Some words are homographs -- same spelling, different meaning! First to get 3 in a row wins!
