Teacher's Manual: Reading Champion Review Bingo 🎲
Goal of the Game
Students review all key vocabulary from Unit 1 (Jack's Birthday, Lessons 1-4) in a game format. Reinforces RF.1.2, RF.1.3, and RL.1.1.
How to Play
  1. Teacher draws a calling card and reads the word and clue aloud.
  2. Students find the word on their bingo card and cover it with a marker.
  3. First student to cover 3 in a row shouts BINGO! and reads the 3 words to confirm.
Teacher Script 🗣️
Reading Champions, this is our FINAL BINGO of Unit 1! All the words are from Jack's Birthday. I'll give you a clue -- find the word and cover it. Show me everything you learned this unit! First to get 3 in a row wins!
