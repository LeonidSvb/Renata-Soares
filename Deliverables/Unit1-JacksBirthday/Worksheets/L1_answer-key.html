Teacher Answer Key: Lesson 1 Practice Worksheet -- Jack's Birthday

Lesson Plan Guidance: This key is for the Lesson 1 Practice Worksheet. All answers come from the story on Page 5. Accept spelling variations as long as the answer is recognizable. For Q3, accept any version of 'Happy Birthday, Jack!' as correct.

📖 EXERCISE 1: READ, FIND AND WRITE — Answers
1. What does Jack see in his room?
The student should write: a big box
Text Evidence: "He sees a big box in his room." (Page 5)
2. What is inside the box?
The student should write: a bright red boat
Text Evidence: "There is a bright red boat inside." (Page 5)
3. What does the note say?
The student should write: Happy Birthday, Jack!
Text Evidence: "It says, Happy Birthday, Jack!" (Page 5)
🎨 EXERCISE 2: DRAW AND LABEL — Answer
Draw Jack opening his box. Label 2 things in your picture!
The student's drawing should show: Jack standing near or opening the big box, with the bright red boat visible inside
Label 1 (What Jack found:): a bright red boat (accept: boat)
Label 2 (Color of the paper:): brown (accept: brown paper)
